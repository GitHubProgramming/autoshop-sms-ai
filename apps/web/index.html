FAQ

Common
Questions.
Direct
Answers.

Shop owners ask these before they start. The answers are short because the product is straightforward.

Start Free Trial
Will this change how my shop operates? +
No. The system runs entirely in the background. Your team answers calls the same way they always have. When a call is missed, the system handles it automatically. The only thing that changes is appointments appear in your calendar that would have previously been lost. Your staff doesn't interact with the system at all.
Do I need to train my staff? +
No training required. Your service writers will simply see new appointments on their calendar. The onboarding process takes under 20 minutes and is handled by your account setup — not your team.
What if the AI says something wrong? +
The AI is trained specifically for auto repair appointment scheduling. It does not diagnose vehicles, give technical advice, or make commitments beyond booking a time slot. If a customer asks something outside its scope, it tells them a team member will follow up. Every conversation is logged in your dashboard for your review.
Will customers find this annoying? +
The data says no. 94% of SMS messages are opened within 3 minutes. Customers who just tried to call a business are actively looking for a response. A text that shows up within 10 seconds of their call is received as attentive service, not an intrusion. You can customize the opening message to match your shop's voice.
Is this replacing my front desk? +
No. This handles calls your front desk physically cannot answer — during rush periods, lunch, and after hours. It is not a replacement. It is the coverage that currently does not exist. Your service writer stays focused on customers in the shop rather than chasing phone calls.
How long does setup take? +
Setup takes four steps and less than 20 minutes: create your account, connect Google Calendar, activate your SMS number, and confirm your shop name. Your first missed call triggers automatically after that. No technical knowledge required.
