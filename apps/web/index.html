FAQ

Common Questions

Does this replace my staff? +
No. This handles calls your staff physically cannot answer — during rush periods, lunch, and after hours. We provide a dedicated AI line that connects to your current phone flow. No new daily workflow for your team. The only difference is that missed calls turn into booked appointments instead of lost revenue.
Will customers get annoyed? +
Your customers keep calling the same shop number they always have. When a call is missed, they receive a text within 10 seconds — which is received as attentive service, not spam. 94% of SMS messages are read within 3 minutes.
Do I need training? +
No training required. We handle the AI line setup and walk you through connecting it to your phone flow — takes under 20 minutes. Your service writers simply see new appointments appear on the calendar. No new tools to learn.
What if the AI makes a mistake? +
The AI only handles appointment scheduling. It does not diagnose vehicles or give technical advice. If a question falls outside its scope, it tells the customer a team member will follow up. Every conversation is logged in your dashboard.
