Most business phone systems let you set up call forwarding from their web portal or app.

RingCentralSettings → Phone → Call Forwarding → "Forward when unanswered" → enter your AI number
OomaMy Ooma → Preferences → Voicemail & Forwarding → Forward Calls → enter your AI number
VonageDashboard → Numbers → Manage → Call Forwarding → enter your AI number
GrasshopperSettings → Extensions → Forwarding → Add forwarding number → enter your AI number
Google VoiceSettings → Calls → Call Forwarding → Forward to another number → enter your AI number
Other systems:
Log in to your provider's web portal. Look for "Call Forwarding", "Unanswered calls", or "No answer forwarding". Set it to forward to your AI number after 4–5 rings.
